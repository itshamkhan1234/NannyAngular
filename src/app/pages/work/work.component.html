Kctickets.online
Overview

It’s an amazon of events services & tickets booking. This platform enables you to find the varieties of events such as movies, shows, concerts, rallies, fundraiser, convention, classes, dance, festival, party, seminar, social gatherings, etc happening around your city or any city of your choice. At the same time, it enables the event organizers to list their events on the platform and increase their discoverability that eventually leads to more sales.

Our Role

The client already had a successful website but he wanted to refurbish this with modern technology, beautiful looking UI, fast loading web pages, friendly user interface and a platform that can handle large traffics without much hassle. It’s always difficult to migrate a project from a legacy technology to modern but our team did a great job in turning this around. Within 6 weeks our team has built the entire website from scratch, migrated the user base and launched the website without any breakdowns.

IOTech
Overview

IoTech is the leading service & infra provider for automated monitoring solutions for the warehouses, farms, manufacturing plants, inventory and employees. The monitoring solutions are driven by embedded IoT devices at the location that continuously push data to the server and a solid infrastructure at the backend that store, process and analyze the data for the management. An interactive dashboard helps with KPIs, actionable insights & reporting and mobile apps for easy controls, alerts & notifications.

Our Role

Our client has been a leading provider of monitoring solutions for farms in Nigeria and they wanted to expand their operations to other verticals. So, we built a robust technology enabled product with embedded IoT devices at the site and a data rich platform at the backend that ensures reliable data collection, interactive dashboard for KPIs and a SaaS architecture that enabled him to plug & play and distribute the solution to others as well.

Tech Stack
  • Frontend: Angular 8, HTML, CSS
  • Backend: Node JS
  • Database: MySQL
  • Frontend: Protocol: MQTT
  • Cloud: AWS
