Know more about NFT

An NFT token is non-fungible, meaning one coin or token cannot be exchanged for another, unlike a dollar bill. Because of this, it is unique and irreplaceable, just like vintage paintings like the Mona Lisa.

In terms of technology, NFTs are a part of the Ethereum blockchain, and all their information, such as prices, trading histories, meta data, and images is stored on the blockchain. NFT can be built from anything digital, such as images, in-game assets, tweets, videos, and virtual items like clothing, cars, and real estate.

NFT is a revolutionary technology and it marks the beginning of the Metaverse.
