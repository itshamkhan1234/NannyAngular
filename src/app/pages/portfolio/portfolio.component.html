Everlens (NFT for Instagram)
Overview

Everlens allows users to monetize their Instagram posts via an NFT marketplace on Binance Smart Chain. By linking their Instagram accounts, users can fetch their posts from Instagram and convert them into NFTs, place them on the marketplace, and begin receiving offers. Additionally, it offers a custom token, BEP 721, BEP 1155, Metamask wallet, Royalties, Token Transfers, Users Activity, and Wallet management.

Our Role

As a result of the success of Opensea, and revolutionary NFT technology, our client wanted to make it easy for anyone to monetize their content by providing inexpensive minting, better discovery of NFTs, good offers, efficient buying/selling, and so on. Despite the limited help available on the internet we built this marketplace from the ground up in record time.

  • Smart Contracts: Solidity
  • Storage: IPFS
  • Backend: Node JS
  • Cloud: Digital Ocean
